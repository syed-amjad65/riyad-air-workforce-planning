## 🎯 Project Objective

This prototype showcases the ability to:

✅ **Transform complex operational data** into clear, strategic workforce decisions  
✅ **Build forecasting models** for crew, engineering, and ground operations headcount  
✅ **Optimize resource allocation** while maintaining regulatory compliance (GACA/IATA)  
✅ **Create executive-ready dashboards** that support budgeting and scenario planning  
✅ **Scale analytics capabilities** to support fleet growth and network expansion  

*This is a conceptual demonstration using synthetic data — not affiliated with Riyadh Air.*

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔮 Demand Forecasting Engine
- Predicts workforce requirements based on flight hour growth scenarios
- Incorporates attrition rates, training lead times, and seasonal demand patterns
- Supports short-term (3mo), medium-term (12mo), and long-term (3yr) planning horizons

### 💰 Cost Modeling & Optimization
- Calculates labor cost impact of fleet expansion (A321neo / B787)
- Models overtime, recruitment, and training cost variables
- Identifies optimization opportunities through scenario analysis

### 📊 Interactive Executive Dashboard
- Real-time visualization of workforce KPIs via Streamlit
- Scenario sliders for growth rate, cost inflation, and attrition assumptions
- Drill-down capability by department: Crew, Engineering, Ground Operations

### ⚖️ Compliance & Risk Monitoring
- Flags potential regulatory risks (duty time limits, certification gaps)
- Tracks attrition risk index by role category
- Generates compliance readiness reports for audit preparation

## 💡 Strategic Insights (Sample Output)

> Based on synthetic scenario modeling, this prototype generates insights such as:

| Insight | Business Impact |
|---------|----------------|
| 📈 A 10% increase in flight hours requires a 12% increase in crew headcount due to training lead times and reserve buffers | Informs recruitment timeline and budget allocation |
| 💸 Shifting 15% of ground ops to automated scheduling reduces overtime costs by ~8% annually | Supports ROI case for digital transformation investments |
| ⚠️ Current attrition models suggest a risk gap in Engineering roles by Q3 2026 if retention initiatives are not implemented | Enables proactive talent strategy before operational impact |
| 🌍 Fleet expansion to 100+ destinations requires modular workforce planning with regional hub flexibility | Aligns resource strategy with network growth roadmap |

*These insights demonstrate analytical thinking — actual results would use real operational data.*
